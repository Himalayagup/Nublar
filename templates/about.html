Nublar

About Us

Nublar is a lightweight and powerful backend server built in Python, designed to make web development simpler and faster. Whether you're serving static files, building REST APIs, or managing JSON responses, Nublar delivers a minimal yet robust developer experience.

Inspired by the simplicity of Flask and the speed of modern microservices, Nublar helps you spin up reliable services with ease. It's ideal for rapid prototyping, educational purposes, or full-scale microservices where control and customization matter.

Our mission is to give developers a hassle-free backend experience, combining performance, flexibility, and readability.
